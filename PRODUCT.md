# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, editors, content teams, product-related teams, and potential collaborators who need to understand Du Bohan's background quickly while reviewing candidates in Chinese hiring contexts.

## Product Purpose

This site presents Du Bohan's writing, podcast, media research, and AI-related work in Chinese. It should let a visitor understand the profile within 30-60 seconds, open representative writing within one click, open AI projects within one click, and access the resume and LinkedIn profile without digging through the page.

The site must stay easy to maintain. Profile copy, writing entries, project entries, and key links live in editable JSON files so the owner can update content without touching page code.

## Brand Personality

Clear, credible, flexible.

The tone should feel job-oriented without overcommitting to one narrow career track. It should communicate a person who can work across content, communication, research, podcasting, and AI-enabled projects.

## Anti-references

This should not look like a dark hacker portfolio, an animation-heavy creative portfolio, a graphic designer showcase, or a one-track media resume. Avoid exaggerated scrolling effects, decorative gimmicks, and over-specific claims that could constrain broad job applications.

Do not include the incorrect IAMCR experience from the original PRD. Keep unverified achievements out of the interface until the site owner adds them deliberately.

## Design Principles

Lead with legibility. Recruiters should scan the page quickly and understand the profile before committing to deeper reading.

Keep identity flexible. The homepage should frame capabilities rather than lock the owner into a single job title or industry.

Make the real work easy to reach. Writing and AI projects should be visible from the first screen and reachable in one click.

Prefer quiet confidence. Visual design should feel polished and deliberate, but not theatrical.

Make updates low-friction. New work should be added through JSON data and public assets instead of page rewrites.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, clear focus states, semantic headings, keyboard-accessible navigation, responsive layouts for mobile and desktop, and reduced-motion support. Text should remain readable in Chinese on common Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android system fonts.
